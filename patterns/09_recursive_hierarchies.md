# Recursive Hierarchies

## Use When

You need to traverse manager chains, org trees, category trees, or graph-like parent-child structures.

## Core Trick

Build:

- a base case
- a recursive step
- a stop condition implied by no more matching parents or children

## Worked Ideas

- employee to CEO chain
- direct and indirect reports
- category depth expansion

## Interview Note

Say what prevents infinite recursion.
