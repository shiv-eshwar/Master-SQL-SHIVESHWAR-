# Week 9: Interview Patterns Part 2

## The Scary Patterns

These are the patterns that make people feel SQL-hard problems are magical. They are not magical. They are structured.

## Patterns This Week

- gaps and islands
- sessionization
- cohort retention
- funnel analysis
- recursive hierarchies
- streaks

## Core Insight

Most of these patterns depend on turning event streams into labeled groups. Once the groups exist, the rest becomes aggregation.

## Interview Lens

If you can explain the grouping trick, you can solve the query. Do not try to memorize complete solutions word for word.
